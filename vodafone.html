Super Powers:

What is drivexone?

Drivexone is an innovative Vodafone solution that, through the installation of a small device, enables communication from your car to your smartphone, with the aid of a simple app. With Drivexone App, you’ll always know where your car is, you’ll be able to check past routes and receive notifications from your car, for example in case of a low battery.

About the project

Steezie was asked to design a smartphone app for Vodafone’s brand new XONE beta product drivexone. The mission was to (re)design the UX and UI of the existing Vodafone b2b app for iOS and Android. The agile scrum methodology was used and a small team of developers and testers got assigned to the project. I worked together with the team at the Vodafone campus in Dusseldorf.

The process

Concept

You will be able to recognise me by my big yellow ZAP BOOK. This is my trusted old friend and I don't leave the house without it. I always start sketching my ideas on paper, it's faster and easier to find the right look and feel. I start with thumbnails to find the right flow and layout. And from there on I sketch everything on A3 paper.

Prototype

After finalizing the flow and layout, I create an interactive prototype. I use different software for this process depending on what I'm designing for. In this case, I used an iPhone app called POP (prototype on paper).

Icon & badge designs

I was in charge of the art direction and I came up with a style for the badges and icons. The style was liked so much that it is now being used as a foundation for future Vodafone global branding.

Measurements & assets

15 pages specifications document, 300+ assets, 20+ icons, and 40+ unique badges were created.
