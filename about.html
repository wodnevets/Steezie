Steezie Your Design Hero

Steven Dow aka Steezie is an Art director, visual designer and UX designer from Amsterdam The Netherlands.

He has more than 15 years work experience and has designed online and offline for brands in the top 100 at both agency and client side. He enjoys working for BIG and small companies designing concepts and visuals for web sites and web & mobile applications with the goal, providing innovative ideas and designs that solve problems, between businesses and their customers.

“My approach to design doesn't end at making visuals, I also focus on the feel and function my designs. Great design is not purely based on how it looks, it's a combination of stunning visuals, great experience and simplicity. I believe strongly that great design is in the small details.”
